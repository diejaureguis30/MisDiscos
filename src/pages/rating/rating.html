rating Olivia stabler I've been an Eminem fan from day one but hadn't purchased any of his albums, so this was a no brainer for me - it includes all of his best singles from his debut up until 2005 when this was released. My personal favorites are "The Way I Am," his song about telling the media to f*** off (I'm sure most celebrities find this to be their anthem), "Guilty Conscience" featuring Dr. Dre, "Just Lose It," "Without Me," and "Mockingbird" (a heartfelt song for his daughter). For me, every song on this album is fantastic and familiar and there's no filler. Obviously, since it's a greatest hits CD, you already know what you're getting as far as the older material, but the real gem on here is the new song "When I'm Gone." It's a more serious song about how his life as a rapper interferes with his family life since he's gone all the time and he uses great imagery to portray how it negatively affects his daughter. As a fan, it makes you appreciate the sacrifices made to put music out for us and think about it in a way you haven't before. This is an awesome CD with all his greatest singles and few new tunes too! Highly recommended!
